WHAT IS THIS WEBSITE ALL ABOUT EXACTLY?

This website is a platform designed to source, organize and distribute world's knowledge base as meaningfully as possible...for the benefit of mankind. It iss regarded as a Knowledge-Sharing App (contrary to typical Photo or Video-sharing Platforms). The platform is geared towards factual information and social journalism.

HOW IT WORKS

You can think of this website like this: imagine a really really remote, but extremely beautiful and technologically advanced island...like no other. People find their way to the island to share their findings; find & commune with other people that matter to their lives; and add to their personal knowledge base.

Every information is organized into NICHES. Every niche is a virtual 'room' having a public 'dicussion table' ; Members, Private Conversation Centers and Library of High Quality Resources

A user of this platform essentially interracts with three things: Network of friends, Comunities of people (Niches) and Knowledge Cards (in the form of posts).
